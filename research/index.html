Repetitions of Pak-Stanley labels in \(G\)-Shi arrangements

Mathematics Unpublished Discrete Math
The key insight of our work is the introduction of a combinatorial model called the Three Rows Game. Using this model, we develop a classification theorem for the multiplicities of the Pak-Stanley labels of the regions in the \(P_n\)-Shi arrangement, where \(P_n\) is the path graph on \(n\) vertices.
