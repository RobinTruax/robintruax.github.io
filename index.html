I'm interested in epistemology and feminist & political philosophy.

My primary research interest is epistemology.³ I am interested in a number of questions in social epistemology and metaepistemology. These include questions about hermeneutical injustices of dilution and monumentalization, the possibility of collective zetetic norms and inquisitive reasons, and, most recently, how moral encroachment can be used to make sense of intuitions regarding sexual consent without engendering agential demotion.
I am also interested in questions about sexuality and gender as they arise in feminist and political philosophy. These identities and experiences are at once irreducibly individual and deeply entangled with political conditions. I am writing a book on this paradox — on making sense of trans womanhood and trans lesbians in a world profoundly hostile to both.
