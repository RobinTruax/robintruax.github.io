After Oxford, I'll begin a PhD in philosophy at the University of California, Berkeley.

I'm interested in epistemology, logic, and language.

My primary research interest is epistemology, the philosophical study of knowledge, evidence, and justified belief. On one hand, I am interested in a number of questions in "traditional epistemology", especially in social epistemology and metaepistemology, broadly construed, and in areas of epistemology that intersect with the the philosophy of language.

On the other hand, a background in pure math and theoretical computer science has made me sensitive to the bilateral relationship between traditional and formal epistemology. I am particularly interested in understanding how various philosophical commitments manifest as formal axioms and definitions in the language of epistemic logic.
