Lāl salām. I am Roshni¹ Junia Thangavelu Truax²—Roshni Junia for short.

I am a PhD student in philosophy at the University of California, Berkeley. My interests are in transfeminist epistemology, metaphysics, and ethics—in less technical terms, the past, present, and future of gender and sex. I also write queer and trans science fiction. Previously, I studied philosophy, computer science, mathematics, and political science at Stanford University, and Philosophy at the University of Oxford. |
